Personal Life and Family

My name is Chris. I am 24 years old and originally from Lees Summit, Missouri and now I currently reside in Neosho, Missouri with my husband Jackson Lewis-Hite and two Dogs Winnie and Rowdy.

Jackson and I have been together for 8 years and married for 2. I currently work for La-Z-boy at that their manufacturing facility in Neosho as an upholsterer. My husband is a CIS instructor at OTC, so I have decided to take advantage of the free tuition perks from that and take my first coding class.

Over the summer, we spent much of our time finishing half of our basement to move our gameroom/entertainment room downstairs. We were able to complete oneside with the help of a few friends, and family. Our plans are to finish the other side to include a bar and table top board game area. My hobbies include, but are not limited to playing video games, restoring old tractors and soon a truck, roller skating, baking, and spending time with our two dogs.
